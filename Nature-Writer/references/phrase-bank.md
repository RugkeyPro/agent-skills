# Nature 论文常用短语库

按功能分类，所有短语均来源于真实 Nature 系列论文语料。

---

## 一、建立研究重要性（Introduction P1）

### 全球/宏观重要性
- `X has become a growing concern due to its [adverse/critical/profound] effects on [ecosystem/human health/food security].`
- `X is ubiquitous [in/across] [environment/ecosystem/region] and poses a significant threat to [Y].`
- `X represents one of the most pressing environmental challenges of our time.`
- `With [global production/use/emissions] exceeding [X] per year, [topic] has attracted increasing attention.`
- `[Topic] plays an extraordinary role in [ecological/biogeochemical/socioeconomic] processes.`
- `Coastal zones constitute one of the most heavily populated and developed land zones in the world.`
- `[Fisheries/Plastics/Pesticides] are [increasingly used/widely recognized] as [description], with [quantitative scale].`

### 经济/社会重要性
- `[Topic] livelihoods of between [X] and [Y] million people, directly or indirectly.`
- `[X] contributes [X%] of the animal protein needs of [Y billion] people.`
- `[Topic] has high regional exploitation rates, especially in [region].`

---

## 二、研究缺口表达（Introduction P3）

### 核心缺口句
- `However, [key aspect] remains largely unknown / poorly understood / unexplored.`
- `Despite [growing evidence/recent advances], [gap] has received much less attention.`
- `Nevertheless, [quantitative/global/mechanistic] understanding of [X] is lacking.`
- `Yet, no [global/comprehensive/systematic] assessment of [X] exists to date.`
- `[A] is well-established; however, [B] is far less well understood.`
- `This gap in knowledge severely hinders our ability to [manage/predict/mitigate] [X].`
- `Currently, [models/estimates/studies] focus on [A] while [B] remains uncharacterized.`
- `Present estimates vary by [X] orders of magnitude, highlighting the uncertainty.`
- `There is no standard approach for [X], making comparisons across studies challenging.`

### 强调数据稀缺
- `Only a few [local/regional] studies have reported [X]; a global assessment is lacking.`
- `Monitoring [X] is challenging, and a detailed picture of [Y] is largely missing.`
- `Field measurements are scarce and cover limited spatial/temporal ranges.`
- `[X] data are available for only ~[number] sites compared to [large number] globally.`

---

## 三、提出研究目标（Introduction P5）

### "Here, we..." 核心句型
- `Here, we present a global model of [X] based on [data/approach].`
- `Here, we develop a process-based model to assess [X].`
- `Here, we use [method] to [objective], providing [outcome].`
- `Here, we conduct [analysis] to [goal] and draw [implication].`
- `In this study, we employ [X] to assess/investigate/quantify [Y].`
- `To address this gap, we constructed [dataset/model/framework] that [capability].`
- `We aim to demonstrate how [X] will affect [Y] using [approach].`

### 声明贡献新颖性
- `To our knowledge, this is the first [global/spatial/temporal] estimate of [X].`
- `Unlike previous approaches, our method explicitly accounts for [feature].`
- `This study provides, for the first time, [quantitative/global/mechanistic] insight into [X].`
- `Our approach bridges the gap between [A] and [B].`

---

## 四、描述数据与方法

### 数据来源
- `Data were obtained from [database/survey] conducted during [period].`
- `[X] data were downloaded from [source website/database].`
- `We sourced [data] from [reference], which contains [X] records across [Y] countries.`
- `Occurrence records were extracted from [GBIF/OBIS/literature] and cleaned to remove [duplicates/errors].`

### 模型设置
- `The model was parameterized with [variables] from [source].`
- `We used [X] of the records for training and the remaining [Y]% as the test set.`
- `The spatial resolution was standardized to [X]° × [Y]° for model inclusion.`
- `Maximum number of iterations was set to [X] as suggested by [ref].`
- `All input data were processed in [software] to calculate [seasonal averages/statistics].`

### 模型评估
- `Model performance was evaluated using [AUC/RMSE/r²/TSS], with values of [X] indicating [interpretation].`
- `The model achieved [AUC/r²] values above [threshold], indicating [high predictive performance/strong fit].`
- `We validated the model against independent observations from [source], finding [good/reasonable] agreement.`
- `Modelled concentrations generally underestimated observations, [explaining why].`

### 不确定性描述
- `[X] values varied by [X] orders of magnitude due to [source of uncertainty].`
- `The estimated [X] has a [95%/68%] confidence interval of [range].`
- `These estimates are conservative because [reason].`
- `We acknowledge that [limitation] may affect our results; however, [mitigation].`

## 五、结果表述

### 核心发现句
- `We find/found that [X] accounts for [X%] of [total Y] annually.`
- `Rivers annually deliver [X] Mg of [substance] to [recipient], [X]-fold greater than [alternative].`
- `Our analysis shows that [X%] of the world's [beaches/rivers/habitats] are [eroding/suitable/affected].`
- `The top [X] [rivers/species/regions], mostly located in [area], account for [X%] of [total].`
- `[Variable A] exhibited the greatest [decline/increase] of [X–X%].`
- `[Species/factor] showed significant variations between [scenarios/seasons/groups] (p = X).`
- `Maximum and minimum [values] were observed in [condition A] and [condition B], respectively.`

### 空间与地理分布
- `[Hotspots/High concentrations] are located in [specific regions/rivers/countries].`
- `[X] showed a distinct latitudinal/longitudinal pattern, with [description].`
- `The largest [source/contributor] is [X], followed by [Y] and [Z].`
- `Rivers in [region] received [more/less] [pollutant/input] compared to [other regions].`

### 时间与季节变化
- `[X] showed strong seasonal variability, peaking in [month/season] with [value].`
- `More than [X%] of total [Y] occurs between [months A] and [months B].`
- `During [wet season/spring flood], [X] was [X]-fold higher than in [dry season].`
- `High flow events are responsible for a disproportionately large percentage of [X].`

## 六、讨论表达

### 与前人研究对比
- `Our findings are consistent with [ref], who/which [finding].`
- `In contrast to [previous estimate], our [model/analysis] suggests [different result].`
- `This is in line with [ref], who reported [similar result] in [study system].`
- `Our estimate is [X]-fold [higher/lower] than [ref], because [reason].`
- `These results are consistent with the findings of [ref] on [similar system/species].`
- `Previous work has [demonstrated/suggested/shown] [X]; our study extends this by [contribution].`

### 机制解释
- `This may be because [mechanism].`
- `This could be attributed to [driver/process].`
- `This is likely driven by [phytoplankton size structure / temperature increase / monsoon].`
- `The [pattern/difference] probably reflects [underlying mechanism].`
- `[X] is expected to [outcome] since [reason].`
- `The [strong/weak] relationship between [A] and [B] indicates [interpretation].`

### 更广泛影响
- `These findings suggest that [broad implication].`
- `Our results highlight the [critical/pivotal/underestimated] role of [X] in [Y].`
- `[Findings] emphasize the importance of [action/consideration] for [goal].`
- `[X] could [exacerbate/intensify/alleviate] [Y], particularly in [region/scenario].`
- `These results have important implications for [management/policy/conservation].`

### 局限性承认
- `Several limitations apply to this work.`
- `Our estimates are likely [conservative/underestimated] because [reason].`
- `[X] was not explicitly accounted for due to [lack of data/model constraints].`
- `The [coarse resolution/simplified assumptions] of our model means that [limitation].`
- `Future research should incorporate [X] to refine [estimate/model/understanding].`

---

## 七、结论表达

- `In summary, we present [global/first/quantitative] evidence that [finding].`
- `Taken together, our findings demonstrate [key conclusion].`
- `We project that [X] will [increase/decrease/shift] by [X%] under [scenario] by [year].`
- `Our study provides a baseline for [future assessment/monitoring/management].`
- `These results call for [policy action/adaptive management/international cooperation].`
- `Both our methodology and model predictions can be refined once more data are available.`
- `Future improvements require [better data / more observations / higher-resolution models].`

---

## 八、气候变化/未来情景相关

- `Under [RCP 2.6 / SSP 5-8.5 / future scenarios], [species/habitat] would prefer [conditions].`
- `With increasing [temperature/RCP], [species] would shift towards [higher latitudes / deeper waters].`
- `Climate change is expected to [reduce/expand/shift] suitable habitats for [species].`
- `A gradual poleward distribution expansion was predicted for [species] across RCP scenarios.`
- `These changes could have important implications for [ecosystem services/fisheries/biodiversity].`
- `Our projections suggest that [negative/positive] effects from climate change will [intensify/persist].`
- `The precautionary principle suggests [fishing activities / human uses] should be limited in [areas].`
